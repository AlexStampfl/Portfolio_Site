FAQ


How long is a massage usually
A massage is either 60 minutes (1 hour) or 90 minutes (1.5 hour)

What is the difference between a swedish and a deep tissue massage?
Swedish is light and relaxing, deep tissue is more intense, usually for chronic issues

What are the benefits of massage?
Massage can help improve circulation throughout the body. It helps muscles relax and loosen up. And it feels good!

What are knots?
Knots are muscle tissue that, due to repetitive stress, become inflamed and hardened.

How often should I get a massage?
The sweet spot is about 1-2 times a month
